Black Diamond Cocktails started with the idea that today's cocktails were just the beginning.

We saw today's explosion of flavors, ingredients, cultures, and preparations as the raw material for new types of alcohol. New spirits like sake, ourzo, and mate became staples of the American bar, and a renaissance in craft liquors and brews unfolded. From molecular gastronomy to old-school mixology, it's a whole new horizon to reinvent the cocktail.

Background

Some romanticized story about how we were inspired one day in college. Probably tapping into mythologies of craftsmanship and artisanship. We went on a soul-searching adventure around the world finding all these cool, hip liquors that literally changed our lives. Then we came back and decided "Eureka!" why not appropriate a bunch of cultures and make money off of it?

Our Process

First we find a great bartender. They're usually doing really innovative work in some podunk area of the country. Then we trick them into delivering some beautiful piece of craftsmanship. Then we steal all their work, claim it for our own, desecrate all the care with blatant commercialization, and then package it to sell to you direct. Voila. Small-batch, hand-crafted exploitation.
